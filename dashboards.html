Power BI Projects

Below is a selection of analytics dashboards I have built. Each project includes the business problem, domain knowledge, data modeling approach, and key insights derived from the analysis. The dashboards also feature AI-powered chat assistance that allows users to interact with the analysis, ask questions about the data, and explore deeper insights directly from the dashboard.

Car Dealership Sales Performance Dashboard

Domain: Automotive Sales Analytics

Tools: Power BI, DAX, Data Modeling

The car dealership lacks a centralized system to monitor and analyze sales performance across different car models, regions, colors, and body styles. Without clear visibility into key metrics such as total sales, average price, and cars sold over time, management struggles to track trends, evaluate performance, and make data-driven decisions to improve sales growth. A dynamic Power BI dashboard is needed to provide real-time insights and support strategic decision-making.

Insurance Risk and Claims Analysis Dashboard

Domain: Insurance Risk Analytics

Tools: Power BI, DAX, Data Modeling

The insurance company lacks a comprehensive system to assess and monitor risk exposure across policyholders, claims frequency, and financial impacts. Without clear visibility into key metrics such as claim rates, risk scores, and loss ratios over time, management struggles to identify high-risk segments, optimize pricing strategies, and mitigate losses. A dynamic Power BI dashboard is needed to provide real-time risk insights and support data-driven underwriting and claims management decisions.
